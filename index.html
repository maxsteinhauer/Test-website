About

Design as a
strategic lever.

I'm Max — a Design Consultant who sits at the intersection of business strategy and design practice. My work focuses on making that connection deliberate and productive rather than accidental.

Over the years I've worked with organisations navigating growth, transformation, and the question of what they actually stand for. I translate between the language of business and the logic of design — so neither side gets lost in translation.

I believe good design is a consequence of clear thinking. And clear thinking is a discipline that can be taught, scaled, and embedded.
